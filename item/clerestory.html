A journal of the arts

Clerestory is one of the oldest literary magazines on College Hill in Providence. We received submissions of visual art, music, prose, and poetry from Brown and RISD. As Design Editor, I planned, designed, and printed each issue around the pieces our staff selected.

The journal reinvents itself each time it is published. We leave the format at 7" by 5" and the cover in black and white, but otherwise we use the design tell a new story each time.
